Juja is a town in Kiambu county.
The town is located about 30 kilometers North of Nairobi between Thika and Ruiru (other towns in Kiambu). 
The Nairobi Business Park is found in the environs of Juja.
It is also home to The Juja City Mall, Juja Preparatory School, Kalimoni Primary School, 
Mang'u High School and parts of Thika superhighway.
