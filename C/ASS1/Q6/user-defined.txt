- User-defined signals in Unix-like operating systems allow users to create and send custom signals to processes. 
- They are identified by integers between 1 and 31. 
- While common signals like SIGINT (2) or SIGTERM (15) are predefined and widely used, 
- user-defined signals are not bound to specific meanings and can be utilized creatively
